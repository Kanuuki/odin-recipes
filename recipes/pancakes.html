Ingredients:

  • 1 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

Directions:

  1. Step 1: Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Step 2: Combine all ingredients in a blender or with whisk
  3. Step 3: Pour batter onto skillet. Flip pancakes when edges harden. Cook on other side until golden brown
  4. Step 4: Serve with butter and syrup
