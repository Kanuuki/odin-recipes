Orange Chicken

This is one of my all time favorite foods. If I could I'd live off of this for the rest of my life.

This food is most ofen known as a variation of General Tso's chicken in North America rather than in mainland China. The chef named Andy Kao claims to have developed the original Chinese-American oragne chicken recipe at a Panda Express in Hawai'i in 1987.
