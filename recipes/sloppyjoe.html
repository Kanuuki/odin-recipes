Sloppy Joe

Sloppy joe is meat made out of ground beef and tomato sauce. some times called a Manwich when put together with bread. This is a simple recipe anyone can follow along to.

This mixture of meat and tomato sauce goes by different names: Toasted Deviled Hamburgers, CHopped Meat Sandwiches, Spanish Hamburgers, Hamburg a la Creolo, Beef Mironton, and Minced Beef Spanish Style. Sloppy joe have been mentioned as early as the 1930s. Some believe that the original came from Sioux City, Iowa where a cook named Joe added tomato sauce to his "loose meat" sandwiches. Others say that the original came from Key West, Florida, Sloppy Joe's Bar. Which ever it was, thank goodness it exists!

The list of ingredients' you'll need is shown below:

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 hamburger buns, split

Follow these steps and put everything together:

  1. Step 1: Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Step 2: Put in ground beef and onion
  3. Step 3: Cook and stir until beef is crumbly, and evenly brown
  4. Step 4: Drain and discard any excess grease
  5. Step 5: Stir in all wet ingredients including brown sugar and powders
  6. Step 6: Bring beef to mixture to a boil over high heat
  7. Step 7: Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer until sauce has thickened for 30-40 mins
  8. Step 8: Serve
